We're a Team & Community of Technologists

Women in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a network and community of women. Our mission is to encourage and support women in computing through: outreach, professional development, academic initiatives, and social events.

About Us

What We Do

Social

Come to our fun events like pumpkin painting, our annual holiday party, volleyball, and more. We also have outreach events and collaborate with a variety of organizations.

Academic

We provide academic resources like test review sessions, study groups, registration review, and faculty lunches. We also organize technical workshops and host an annual hackathon.

Professional

We provide industry mentorship, graduate mentorship, and panels. Network with our sponsors at corporate events, corporate receptions, and attend on-site tours.

Our Programs & Events

We host a variety of programs and events. Here are just a few:

Corporate

WiCS organizes corporate events, corporate receptions, and on-site tours with our sponsors as well as events with FoCS sponsors.

Social

The social events in WiCS create a community within the department that helps students have a support system.

Academic

WiCS organizes test review sessions, study groups, registration review, and faculty lunches so that WiCS members feel prepared for their classes.

Technical

WiCS hosts technical workshops to help members learn new skills.

Outreach

Outreach within WiCS organizes volunteer events with local schools teaching young kids about CS and participates in outreach events.
