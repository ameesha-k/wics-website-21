Alison Norman

Faculty Advisor

Dr. Norman is a lecturer in The University of Texas at Austin Computer Science Department, currently teaching courses like Principles of Computer Systems and Ethical Foundations of Computer Science. Her research interests lie in the intersection of supercomputing and program analysis and she is passionate about nuturing the next generation of women STEM leaders.
